This project was created by four Waterloo students as part of Hack the North 2021.


This project was made as a way to tackle one of the main issues of our generation: connections with family. It is often difficult to trace back records of family history without having the physical copy of the family tree. Likewise, it is hard to keep the tree going if the family branches out and you lose contact with extended family members. Find Your Roots enables family members to stay connected, even if physically apart.
