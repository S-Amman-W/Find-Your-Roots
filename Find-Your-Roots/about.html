This project was created by four Waterloo students for the Northern Hackathon of '21.


Meet the team!

This project was made as a way to tackle one of the main issues of our generation, interacting with family. There is so much out there that we often forget about the importance of family. With the implementation of this project, we truly believe that we can make a difference in the way people interact with their families and bond with them.
