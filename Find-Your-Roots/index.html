Find-Your-Roots



A personalized family-tree building software to keep records of family history, learn more about relative's personalities, and stay in touch in today's world.

Family is everything. So we reinvented the way families can be together.

Find Your Roots is a software platform that allows you to share and collaborate on family trees with relatives, and keep in touch through through the Events and Moments channels. It also allows you to learn more about your loved ones through the shared interests, fun facts, and Myers-Briggs Personality sections included in each family member's profile. Whether that's across cities, borders, states, or countries, Find Your Roots helps you keep in touch with those who matter most to you.

It's simple! Login or create an account to get started.
