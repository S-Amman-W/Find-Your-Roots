Find Your Roots



A personalized family-tree building software to keep records of family history, learn more about relative's personalities, and stay in touch in today's world.

Find Your Roots is a software platform that allows you to share and collaborate on family trees with relatives. It also allows you to learn more than just the basic details about your family members through the shared interests, fun facts, and Myers-Briggs Personality sections in people's profiles. Whether that's across cities, borders, states, or countries, Find Your Roots helps you preserve the history and connection with those who matter most to you.

Login or create an account to get started.
